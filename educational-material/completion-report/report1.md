# Milestone 1: Project Setup and Initial Video Production

## Milestone Outputs

Establish the foundational elements for the YouTube educational initiative. This includes detailed project planning, setting up production workflows, and producing the first twelve weekly videos in English, Spanish, and Portuguese. Each video will have a minimum duration of one hour, focusing on introducing Cardano and Midnight tools tailored for web2 and business developers.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Develop comprehensive project plan and content calendar outlining topics and objectives.
- Erick Romero and Tainá Bugs will lead the production process, leveraging their developer expertise, multilingual capabilities, and practical experience from hackathons and Cardano/Midnight initiatives to create clear, developer-focused content with practical demonstrations.
- Tools in our workflow include VS Code for code editing, OBS for screen capturing, Premiere Pro for video editing, along with a camera and microphone.
- First Twelve Videos Produced and Published: 12 high-quality tutorial videos delivered in English, Spanish, and Portuguese.
- Quality will be ensured through practical demonstrations, real-world use cases, and multilingual accessibility, supporting web2 developers in transitioning to full-stack Cardano and Midnight developers and focusing on practical content that drives engagement over time.

## Evidence of Milestone Completion

- The approved project plan and content calendar will be documented and stored in the project repository on GitHub. A link to the repository and documentation will be provided.
- Production workflows and team roles defined and operational.
- The videos will be available on a dedicated channel on YouTube in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, and links to the videos will be provided.

📅 Dynamic Content Calendar:
Given the rapidly evolving nature of the Midnight ecosystem and Cardano's development, we've adopted a dynamic content strategy that allows us to be responsive to the latest developments:

  • Roadmap-Aligned Planning → Content topics are selected based on the Cardano and Midnight roadmaps and thematic focus areas, ensuring relevance and timeliness.
  • Bi-weekly Planning Cycles → We plan content in two-week sprints to remain agile and responsive to the latest ecosystem developments.
  • Community-Driven Topics → We actively incorporate community feedback and questions into our content planning.
  • GitHub Tracking → All content planning and development is tracked in our GitHub repository.

❓ Why No Fixed Calendar?
We've found that maintaining a flexible, responsive approach allows us to provide the most relevant and up-to-date information to our community. This approach ensures we can quickly adapt to new developments in the Midnight and Cardano ecosystems while maintaining educational value and technical accuracy.
